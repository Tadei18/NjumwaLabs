> Real engagement, delivered as a D365 F&O developer at Impax Business Solutions. The client is anonymized for confidentiality — **pending owner approval before naming**.

## The context

A leading East African insurance group was implementing Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations as its core enterprise ERP, serving 200–1,000 users across finance and operations. The rollout spanned six core modules: **General Ledger, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Fixed Assets, Inventory, and Procurement & Sourcing**.

## The problem

Standard D365 F&O rarely matches exactly how an insurer runs its finance and operations. The business needed custom behaviour, clean data migrated from legacy systems, and trustworthy reporting for finance, claims and underwriting — all delivered without compromising Microsoft's continuous upgrade path.

## The approach

As part of the technical team, I delivered components across the full lifecycle — build, test, UAT and hypercare:

- **Upgrade-safe customizations.** Designed and developed custom AOT elements — tables, forms, classes, data entities, and table/form extensions — in **X++ using the Chain of Command pattern**, extending standard F&O behaviour without breaking upgrade paths.
- **Data migration.** Configured **Data Management Framework (DMF)** projects to migrate master data and historical transactions from legacy systems during cutover.
- **Reporting.** Built **SSRS and Power BI** reports surfacing financial and operational KPIs for finance, claims and underwriting teams.
- **Requirements to specs.** Translated business requirements into technical specifications alongside senior architects.
- **Stabilization.** Resolved post-go-live defects and **tuned performance of high-volume transactional workflows** in production.

## The stack

Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations, X++ with the extension framework and Chain of Command, Data Management Framework, SSRS, Power BI, Azure DevOps and Lifecycle Services (LCS).

## The result

The implementation went live to **200+ users across six core modules**, with upgrade-safe customizations, migrated master and historical data, and near real-time KPI visibility for finance, claims and underwriting. Post-go-live, high-volume transactional workflows were stabilized and tuned for production load.
